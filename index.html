About Me

One thing I am passionate about, is helping other people's passions grow. My roughly 14 years in the United States Air Force has taught me to work with others and find solutions to difficult problems. This skill was acquired through earning a Green Belt in Continuous Process Improvement, which just means I facilitated many teams to get to a good solution.

I have also taught others on how to use certain software for job flow and designing/deploying a mobile app for users. This experience coupled with being a Department of Equal Opportunity Management Institute (DEOMI) graduate, which allows me to leverage my skills learned with my Sociology BA to help others find solutions, whether in the job or behavioral challenges within teams.

This lead me to my passion with being a Full Stack Engineer. I have a passion to help other's passions grow-- and what better way then to help in technology. I currently graduated from Code Platoon bootcamp, where I learn languages such as Javascript and Python along with Object Oriented Programming with Django and PostgreSQL.

I apply my knowledge by learning from others. I want to create and develop other's passions to be deployed to others. Whether working on a team or solo, I can get the job done-- and it is the mission, the goal, and the drive that keeps me going.
